# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-21

Stay Ready Family ("the App") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App handles, how it is used, and how you can contact us.

## 1. Information the App handles
The App may handle the following information that you enter directly:
- family member information such as name, relationship, and nationality
- document information such as document type, display title, expiry date, issue date, memo, and reminder settings
- renewal history related to document expiry updates
- document photos that you choose to attach

## 2. How your information is used
Your information is used only to provide the App’s core features, including:
- showing document status and action priority
- scheduling reminders
- saving renewal history
- storing attached document photos
- unlocking paid features after a valid purchase

## 3. Local storage
The App primarily stores your data locally on your device.
This includes family members, documents, reminder settings, renewal history, and attached document photos.

At this time, Stay Ready Family does not require account creation and does not provide mandatory cloud sync.

## 4. Photos and camera access
If you choose to attach a document photo, the App may request access to:
- your photo library
- your camera

These permissions are used only to let you select or capture document images that you choose to save in the App.

## 5. Notifications
If you allow notifications, the App may send local reminders on your device for document-related dates.
These reminders are generated from the information you store in the App.

## 6. In-app purchases
The App offers a paid unlock called `Family Unlock`.
Purchases are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play, not directly by the App developer.
We do not receive your full payment card details.

## 7. Data sharing
We do not sell your personal information.
We do not share your document data with third parties for advertising purposes.

Some platform providers may process limited technical information required for:
- app distribution
- in-app purchase processing
- system notification delivery

## 8. Data deletion
You can delete your data directly inside the App by deleting family members, documents, photos, and related records.
If you uninstall the App, locally stored data on that device may be removed, subject to your device and platform behavior.

## 9. Children
The App is intended as a household document management tool and is not directed specifically to children.

## 10. Changes to this policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
If important changes are made, the updated version will be reflected in the App or related store materials.
