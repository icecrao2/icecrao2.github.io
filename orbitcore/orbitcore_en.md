# Orbit Core Shift Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-15

Orbit Core ("the App") may process limited information to provide gameplay features and advertising functions.

## 1. Information We May Collect
- Run record data
  - survival time
  - level reached
  - enemies defeated
- Device and ad-related data
  - device identifiers or advertising identifiers provided by ad SDKs
  - diagnostic data required for ad requests, impressions, and rewarded ad delivery

The App does not provide user account registration or its own backend account system.

## 2. How We Use Information
- to save and display run history
- to serve ads and process rewarded ad flows
- to monitor stability and analyze errors

## 3. How Information Is Stored
- Run history may be stored locally on the user's device.
- Ad-related information may be processed by advertising providers under their own policies.

## 4. Third-Party Services
The App may use third-party service providers for advertising.

Current or potential third-party services
- Google AdMob

Please review the privacy policies of those providers for details on their own data handling practices.

## 5. Children's Privacy
The App is not specifically designed for children, and settings may be adjusted as required by applicable laws and store policies.

## 6. Your Choices
- You can limit ad identifiers or tracking permissions in your device settings.
- If you delete the App, locally stored run history may also be removed.

## 7. Contact
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please use the contact information provided on the app distribution page.
