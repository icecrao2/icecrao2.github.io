Mandalart Pyramid is provided by Moonworks ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles personal data.

## 1. Data stored on your device

The app stores your mandalart content locally on your device. This includes:

- Life, Yearly, and Monthly mandalart boards
- board history, revisions, and archives
- app settings
- a cached premium entitlement state

The current version does not use cloud sync as the operational source of truth.

## 2. Analytics

In release builds, the app uses Firebase Analytics to measure app usage events such as:

- app opens
- section views
- premium purchase taps
- premium restore taps
- tracking authorization status

We do not intentionally send the text you write inside your mandalart boards as analytics event content.

In debug builds, analytics collection is disabled.

## 3. Tracking permission on iOS

On iOS, the app may ask for App Tracking Transparency permission. If you deny permission, the app will still work, but tracking-dependent analytics behavior may be limited by the platform.

## 4. Purchases

Premium purchases and purchase restores are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play, depending on your platform. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

We may store a secure local cache of your premium entitlement and refresh that entitlement with the relevant store when the app starts or when you manually restore purchases.

## 5. Sharing of data

We may share limited data with:

- Google Firebase Analytics, for analytics processing
- Apple or Google, for purchase and restore verification

We do not sell your personal data.

## 6. Retention

- Local board data remains on your device until you edit it, delete it, clear app data, or uninstall the app.
- Analytics data retention is governed by Google Firebase settings and policies.
- Purchase-related records are retained by the relevant app store under its own policies.

## 7. Your choices

You can:

- delete local app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage
- manage tracking permission in iOS Settings
- use the restore purchase action to re-check prior premium entitlements

## 8. Children

The app is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the app, contact us.
